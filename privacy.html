SureVriksha Financial Advisory ("we," "our," or "us"), operated by Yashraj Jain, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our financial advisory services.

By accessing our website or services, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms, please do not access the site.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide Directly:

  • Full name, date of birth, and gender
  • Contact information: phone number, email address, mailing address
  • Financial information: income, existing insurance policies, investment goals
  • Health information: only when required for health insurance applications
  • Government ID numbers: PAN card, Aadhaar (where legally required for KYC)
  • Nominee information provided for policy applications
  • Communication records: emails, WhatsApp messages, call logs

Information Collected Automatically:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Referring URLs and search terms
  • Device information (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Cookie data and session information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide insurance advisory and financial planning services
  • Process insurance applications and policy renewals on your behalf
  • Assist with insurance claims coordination and settlement
  • Communicate about your policies, renewals, and important updates
  • Send relevant financial planning tips and product recommendations
  • Conduct KYC (Know Your Customer) verification as required by law
  • Comply with IRDAI regulations and other applicable Indian laws
  • Improve our services, website, and client experience
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security of our systems
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)

Legal Basis: We process your data based on (a) performance of a contract or pre-contractual steps, (b) legal obligations under Indian law, (c) your consent, and (d) our legitimate interests in providing advisory services.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Insurance Companies: To process applications, issue policies, and settle claims — this is essential to the service.
  • IRDAI and Regulatory Bodies: As required by Indian insurance regulations and law.
  • Technology Service Providers: Website hosting, CRM tools, and communication platforms that are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by court order, law enforcement request, or applicable law.
  • Business Successors: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets — with advance notice to you.

We require all third parties to respect the security of your personal data and to treat it in accordance with the law.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmitted through our website
  • Secure, access-controlled storage of physical and digital records
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Staff training on data protection and confidentiality
  • Two-factor authentication for internal systems

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by applicable law.

We retain your personal data for as long as your policy is active and for a minimum of 7 years thereafter, as required by IRDAI regulations and the Income Tax Act.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g., Google Analytics). Data is anonymized.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences for future visits.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements. Only with your consent.

You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality. We do not use cookies to collect sensitive financial or health information.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection laws and GDPR principles (where applicable), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for marketing purposes
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for processing at any time (without affecting previous processing)
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@surevriksha.com or call +91 95834 25025. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Third-Party Services & Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites including insurance company portals, government websites (IRDAI, MCA), and payment gateways. These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we have no responsibility or liability for their content or practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any information.

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics (website analytics — privacy.google.com)
  • WhatsApp Business API (client communication)
  • Google Workspace (email and document management)

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately at privacy@surevriksha.com and we will promptly delete such data.

For child insurance plans (which are purchased by parents/guardians), we collect the parent's information and the child's basic details (name, date of birth) required for the policy.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify active clients via email or WhatsApp
  • Post a prominent notice on our website

Your continued use of our services after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
