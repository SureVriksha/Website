The Founder's Story

From a Passion for Finance
to a Mission of Protection

Growing up, I watched my own family struggle after an unexpected medical emergency — not because of the illness, but because they had no financial backup. That moment planted a seed that eventually became SureVriksha.

After completing my education in finance and earning my IRDAI certification, I spent years understanding the complexities of the Indian insurance market. What struck me most was how millions of families were either under-insured or paying for policies that didn't truly serve them.

In 2014, I founded SureVriksha — meaning "Sure Tree" — a symbol of stability, growth, and protection. Like a banyan tree that provides shelter to all, our mission is to be the financial safety net for every Indian family.

Today, SureVriksha has helped over 2,000 families secure their futures, settled hundreds of claims, and built a reputation for honest, expert advice that puts the client first — always.
